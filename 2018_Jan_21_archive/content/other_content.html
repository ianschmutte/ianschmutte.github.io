Visualization of a Job Mobility Network

A subgraph of the realized mobility network in the Panel Study of Income Dynamics (1994-2001) drawn using Pajek v1.16. Each node represents a worker in the sample. An edge connects two workers when they share a pseudo-employer (industry-occupation pair).

U.S. Census Bureau

While in graduate school, I worked for the U.S. Census Bureau. In 2007-2008 I worked with the Longitudinal Employer-Household Dynamics (LEHD) program. From July 2008 until June 2010, I worked for the Center for Economic Studies as the Administrator for the Census Research Data Center at Cornell.
